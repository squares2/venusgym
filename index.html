We offer a comprehensive range of unique sports programs at our academy, designed to develop the physical and mental skills of all age groups.

  • Calisthenics

    Bodyweight training for strength and fitness. Ideal for both children and adults to build strong muscles, increase flexibility, and improve overall motor control.

    Discover More

  • Table Tennis (Ping Pong)

    A fun and fast-paced sport for children and adults. Perfect for improving reaction speed, enhancing mental focus, and refining hand-eye coordination.

    Discover More

    Regular walking adventures on natural trails and in the open air. A fantastic group activity to boost cardio fitness, explore nature, and cultivate a spirit of adventure.

    Discover More

  • Yoga

    Discover balance and inner peace with Yoga sessions specially designed for ladies. We help you increase body flexibility, improve core strength, and relieve stress and anxiety. It is the perfect path to achieving body-mind harmony.

    Discover More

  • Muay Thai

    Learn the art of Thai Boxing (Muay Thai). We focus on discipline, building confidence, and teaching essential self-defense techniques, progressing to professional levels, in a safe and enjoyable manner.
